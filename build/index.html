    Diminished Marginal History




    It's a map with markers that show you some of the history of Amsterdam, of course! You get to choose which history gets to be a part of the 'canon' and which ones get diminished. By law of diminished marginal utility you cannot choose all of them, so you'll have to decide. Will you go for the ones we already know, or will you rewrite history, finding something else important on the map?

    N.B.: Not all functionality is working. The 'Choose' button in the popups is not yet activated, but let's pretend it is.
